Sense of Self

Photography

"my only room"

A series of 8 photographs expressing my sense of self. Taken during Fall of 2020, in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, I had spent the last 8 months in my room. This room is a special place for me filled with an extensive collection of everything I love; I almost consider it a special piece of art. This room, no matter what mood I may be in, always inspires me to someday make art that makes people happy the way my collection makes me feel. Before quarantine, this room was something that I shaped, but now it feels like this room and the comfort it brings is what keeps my shape from crumbling apart. Thus I decided to photograph my room and my daily life in it for this sense of self project.

Mediums
