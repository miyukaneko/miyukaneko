About Me

Born in Tokyo, Raised in Califonia.

Miyu Kaneko is an Digital Media Artist (and Kirby lover) from San Jose. After being born in Japan, she moved to California at three months old. From this biculturality, she adapted an unique art style in mixing American and Japanese pop art-styles. With her struggle to adapt in the two cultures came a strong sense of exploration of self-identity, compassion, and having a curiosity for open conversations about delicate topics of mental health and individuality. She graduated From San Jose State University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Digital Media Art, Spring 2023.

Experienced in Blender, Paint tool SAI, Procreate, Processing, P5.js, HTML, Adobe LightRoom, Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and a bit of MAYA and 3ds Max.
