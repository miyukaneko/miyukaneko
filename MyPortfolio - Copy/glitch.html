"Pink Demon"

A project in dissecting an image and forcing them into applications they do not belong in. Using unknown errors to my advantage to create a new visual aesthetic was challenging yet rewarding. Not knowing what the end result was going to look like until the very end was a new factor in art for me, and I had a lot of fun with this experimental project.
